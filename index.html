    Utkarsh Bajpai

    Hi, I’m Utkarsh, I am a Staff Research Scientist at IBM Research (T. J. Watson Research Center, Yorktown Heights, NY) working on some of the coolest problems in advanced semiconductor technology. Most of my time goes into figuring out how to make next-gen CMOS devices–like super small transitors, going faster and faster each generation. I am a physics nerd and just love learning about new things. This website is a collection of some of my work and thoughts. Take a look and enjoy!
